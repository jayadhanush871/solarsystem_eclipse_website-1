The Solar System consists of the Sun, planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.

The Solar System

The Sun is a star at the center of our Solar System, around which eight planets revolve. These planets include Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. In addition to the planets, our Solar System is home to various moons, asteroids, comets, and dwarf planets.
